About the Book

Music Theory and The Fretboard was created for new and self-taught guitarists who want to finally understand how music fits together on the neck. This book connects music theory to real fretboard shapes through clear visuals and explanations — no unnecessary jargon.

The ebook version includes three bonus sections:

  • Introduction to Standard Notation – Learn to read guitar music.
  • Introduction to Ear Training – Discover solfege and how to develop your musical ear.
  • Level 2 Preview – A first look at triads and intermediate concepts.

Authors

Denisse Vallecillos is Orchestral Conductor and public school educator. She holds degrees in Music Education and Orchestral Conducting, and co-taught a high school guitar class for five years.

Chris Paul holds a Guitar Skills Certificate from Berklee Online and co-authored Music Theory and The Fretboard. His curriculum helped beginners strum along with songs like “Zombie” and “Hotel California” within weeks.
